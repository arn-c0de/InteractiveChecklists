# F-16C Viper Shutdown Checklist

## After Landing Procedure (often done while taxiing)

- [ ] **Speed Brakes:** Close them after braking to taxi speed.
- [ ] **Ejection Seat:** De-arm the ejection seat.
- [ ] **CMDS and RWR:** Ensure Countermeasures Dispenser System (CMDS) and Radar Warning Receiver (RWR) are turned off.
- [ ] **IFF:** Turn the Identification Friend or Foe (IFF) system to OFF.
- [ ] **Pitot Heat:** Turn the Pitot tube heater to OFF.
- [ ] **Radar (FCR):** Turn off the Fire Control Radar (FCR), hardpoints, and radar altimeter.
- [ ] **Landing/Taxi Lights:** Turn off landing and taxi lights once parked.
- [ ] **Armament Switches:** Double-check that all armament switches, including laser and master arm, are OFF.
- [ ] **Parking Brake:** Engage the parking brake.

## Engine Shutdown Procedure

- [ ] **HUD:** Turn off the Head-Up Display (HUD).
- [ ] **Navigation/Avionics/Oxygen Supply:** Turn off navigation, avionics systems, and oxygen supply (SUPPLY to OFF).
- [ ] **JFS and EPU Lights:** Confirm that the JFS (Jet Fuel Starter) and EPU (Emergency Power Unit) indicator lights are extinguished.
- [ ] **Throttle:** Move the throttle to the CUT-OFF position (often by a specific key command in simulators).
- [ ] **RPM Monitoring:** Observe engine RPM until it drops below 20%.
- [ ] **Main Power:** Once RPM is below 20%, turn off the main electrical power switch.
- [ ] **Canopy:** Unlock and open the canopy.
- [ ] **Ground Crew:** Request ground crew to place chocks and disconnect the aircraft battery if accessible.
