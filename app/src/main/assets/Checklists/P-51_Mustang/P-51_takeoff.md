# P-51 Mustang Takeoff Checklist

## Before Takeoff

- [ ] **Trim Tabs:**
    - [ ] Rudder: 5° RIGHT
    - [ ] Elevator: 2° to 3° nose UP (with 25 gallons or less in fuselage tank); 1° to 3° nose DOWN (fuselage tank full)
    - [ ] Aileron: 0°
- [ ] **Oil and Coolant Shutters:** AUTOMATIC
- [ ] **Wing Flaps:** 20°, if desired
- [ ] **Mixture:** AUTO RICH
- [ ] **Propeller:** Full INCREASE RPM
- [ ] **Fuel Booster Pump:** EMERGENCY, check for 14-19 psi
- [ ] **GEN switch (GEN):** ON
- [ ] **Fuel Selector:** To fullest tank (Note: Never land with droppable wing tanks)
- [ ] **Controls:** Unlock and check for freedom of movement
- [ ] **Canopy:** Closed and locked (ensure no red indicators show, which would mean the emergency release is unlocked)
- [ ] **Instruments:** Check for proper readings and uncage all gyro instruments
- [ ] **Communication Equipment:** Check for operation
- [ ] **Armament Switches:** START (if applicable)

## During Takeoff Roll

- [ ] **Throttle:** Advance smoothly to full power.
- [ ] **Engine Instruments:** Monitor for proper indications (Oil Pressure, Oil Temperature, Coolant Temperature).
- [ ] **Directional Control:** Maintain with rudder.
- [ ] **Rotation Speed:** Rotate at appropriate airspeed.

## After Liftoff

- [ ] **Landing Gear:** Retract when a positive rate of climb is established and airspeed is sufficient.
- [ ] **Flaps:** Retract as appropriate for climb.
- [ ] **Booster Pump:** Switch to NORMAL (from EMERGENCY).
