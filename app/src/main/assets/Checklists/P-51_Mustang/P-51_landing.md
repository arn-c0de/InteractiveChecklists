# P-51 Mustang Landing Checklist

## Before Landing

- [ ] **Mixture:** AUTO RICH
- [ ] **Oil and Coolant Shutters:** AUTOMATIC (or OPEN for taxiing)
- [ ] **Fuel Selector:** To fullest tank (Never land with droppable wing tanks)
- [ ] **Booster Pump:** NORMAL
- [ ] **RPM:** Increased to 2600
- [ ] **Trim Tabs:** Set as required (e.g., rudder 5° RIGHT, elevator 2° to 3° nose UP with less than 25 gallons in fuselage tank; elevator 1° to 3° nose DOWN with fuselage tank full; aileron 0°)

## Landing Gear Down

- [ ] **Throttle:** Retard to check landing gear warning light (MP: 17" to 22")
- [ ] **Airspeed:** 170 IAS or below
- [ ] **Landing Gear Handle:** DOWN position
- [ ] **Check Warning Lights:** If light does not come ON at previously noted throttle position, gear is down and locked
    - *Note:* On P-51D, there is no Arens control to prevent accidental raising of gear on the ground. Do not move the landing gear control when the airplane is on the ground, as there is no safety mechanism to prevent retraction.

## Flaps

- [ ] **Flaps:** Lower as desired. Maximum full-flap speed: 165 IAS.
    - *Note:* A stabilized approach typically involves gear down and full flaps.

## After Landing / Shutdown

- [ ] **Flaps:** Raise
- [ ] **Booster Pump:** OFF
- [ ] **Oil and Coolant Shutters:** OPEN
- [ ] **Engine Run:** To 1500 RPM
- [ ] **Mixture Control:** IDLE CUT-OFF
- [ ] **Throttle:** Move fully open
- [ ] **Ignition Switch:** OFF (after propeller stops turning)
- [ ] **Fuel Shut-off Valve:** OFF
- [ ] **Parking Brake:** Set
- [ ] **Controls:** Unlock and check for freedom of movement (Control lock is at the base and forward of the stick; pull plunger on left side to unlock)
- [ ] **Tail Wheel:** Keep stick all the way back after touchdown to lock the tail wheel to 6 degrees of travel. For sharp turns, push stick forward of neutral to allow full swiveling action.
- [ ] **Brakes:** Use gently; avoid jamming to prevent nose-over.
