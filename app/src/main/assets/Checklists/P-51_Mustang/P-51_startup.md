# P-51 Mustang Startup Checklist

## Preliminaries

- [ ] **Ignition switch:** OFF
- [ ] **Parking brake:** SET
- [ ] **Seat and rudders:** Adjust for height and length
- [ ] **Bomb and gun safety switches:** OFF
- [ ] **Landing gear handle:** Ensure in the DOWN position (Note: On P-51D, there is NO ARENS control to prevent accidental raising of gear on the ground)
- [ ] **Controls:** Unlock and check for freedom of movement. The control lock is located at the base and just forward of the stick. Pull plunger on left side of the lock to unlock controls
- [ ] **Safety belt and shoulder straps:** Fasten
- [ ] **Altimeter:** Set to correct barometric pressure
- [ ] **Oil and coolant shutters:** Full OPEN as soon as battery cart is plugged in
- [ ] **Trim tabs:** Set
    - [ ] Rudder: 5° RIGHT
    - [ ] Elevator: 2° to 3° nose UP (with 25 gallons or less in fuselage tank); 1° to 3° nose DOWN (fuselage tank full)
    - [ ] Aileron: 0° for takeoff
- [ ] **Hydraulic pressure:** Release with wing flaps and flap handle to UP position
- [ ] **Canopy (bubble):** To close, push in on axle of crank on right side of cockpit to engage clutch. Disengage pin on crank handle from the holes on the face of the clutch housing by pulling crank knob inboard gently. Turn crank counterclockwise, holding knob inboard to close canopy. WARNING: If red indicators show through openings on each side of the forward end of the enclosure, the emergency release is unlocked and unsafe for flight.

## Starting Procedure

- [ ] **Propeller:** Have prop pulled through, with ignition switch OFF, if plane has been idle more than 2 hours
- [ ] **GEN & BAT switches:** GEN ON, BAT ON (unless battery cart is being used, then BAT OFF)
- [ ] **Throttle:** Open 1 inch
- [ ] **Mixture control:** IDLE CUT-OFF
- [ ] **Propeller control:** INCREASED RPM
- [ ] **Supercharger switch:** AUTOMATIC
- [ ] **Carburetor air control:** RAM AIR
- [ ] **Ignition switch:** Turn to BOTH
- [ ] **Fuel shut-off valve:** ON
- [ ] **Fuel selector valve:** To fuselage tank (if full), or Left Main tank if fuselage tank not used
- [ ] **Fuel booster pump:** NORMAL, check for 8-12 pounds of fuel pressure
- [ ] **Prime engine:** 3 to 4 shots if cold, 1 to 2 if warm
- [ ] **Clear the prop**
- [ ] **Starter switch:** Lift guard and press switch to START. Caution in use of starter not to overheat
- [ ] **As engine starts:** Move mixture control to AUTO RICH
- [ ] **If engine does not fire after several turns:** Continue priming. WARNING: When engine is not firing, mixture control should be in IDLE CUT-OFF
