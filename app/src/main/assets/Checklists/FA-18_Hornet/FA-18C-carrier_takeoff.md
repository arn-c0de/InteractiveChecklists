# FA-18 Hornet Carrier Takeoff Checklist

This checklist covers the procedures for a catapult launch from an aircraft carrier.

---

## Before Catapult Launch

- [ ] **TAXI to Catapult** - Follow ground crew signals.
- [ ] **Launch Bar** - EXTEND
- [ ] **Canopy** - CLOSED and LOCKED
- [ ] **Ejection Seat** - ARMED
- [ ] **Exterior Lights** - As required

---

## On the Catapult

- [ ] **Position on Catapult** - As directed by ground crew.
- [ ] **Shuttle** - Engage launch bar with shuttle.
- [ ] **Brakes** - Release once shuttle is engaged.
- [ ] **Tension** - Ground crew will signal for tension.
- [ ] **Engine Run-up** - Spool engines to MIL power.
- [ ] **Wipeout** - Perform control surface wipeout (check full, free, and correct movement).
- [ ] **Afterburner** - Engage full afterburner when signaled.
- [ ] **Check Engines** - Instruments normal, AB light on.
- [ ] **Salute** - Salute the Catapult Officer when ready for launch.

---

## Catapult Launch Sequence (from PDF)

- [ ] **LAUNCH BAR** - EXTEND
- [ ] **Hook Up** - Follow Directions / U (Up)
- [ ] **BRAKES** - RELEASE
- [ ] **LAUNCH BAR** - RETRACT (This seems to be part of the post-launch sequence in practice, but is listed here in the PDF)
- [ ] **THROTTLE** - MIL/MAX*
> *If > 45,000 lb use MAX.
- [ ] **FLIGHT CONTROLS** - CYCLE
- [ ] **WARN / CAUT LT OF.** - CHECK
- [ ] **ENG INSTS** - CHECK
- [ ] **Hand Signal** - Salute**
> **Super Carrier

---

## After Catapult Launch

- [ ] **Positive Rate of Climb** - Establish
- [ ] **Landing Gear** - UP
- [ ] **Flaps** - AUTO (retract above 250 KIAS)
- [ ] **Throttle** - Adjust as required for climb.
- [ ] **Afterburner** - Disengage when clear of the carrier.
- [ ] **Climb** - To assigned altitude.
- [ ] **ATC** - Contact departure frequency.
