# MiG-21bis Taxi Checklist

## Taxi Procedure

- [ ] **Nose Wheel Lock:** Ensure the nose wheel lock is set to the "OFF" (vertical) position for taxiing.
- [ ] **Landing/Taxi Lights:** If required, set the landing/taxi light switch to the "taxi" (middle) position.
- [ ] **Main Air Pressure:** Verify that the main air pressure gauge reads at least 10 kg/cm².
- [ ] **Canopy:** Confirm that the canopy is properly sealed.
- [ ] **Flaps:** Check that the flaps are set to the takeoff position.
- [ ] **Initiate Taxi:** Increase throttle to approximately 75-80% RPM to begin moving.
- [ ] **Taxi Speed:** Maintain a taxi speed below 50 km/h. For turns, reduce speed to around 20 km/h.
- [ ] **Steering:** Use differential braking for steering. To turn, engage the rudder in the desired direction and then apply the brake lever on the flight stick.
- [ ] **Braking:** Use short presses of the brake. Avoid pressing the brake lever for more than one or two seconds at a time to conserve compressed air.
- [ ] **Approaching Runway:** Line up the aircraft on the runway and roll forward a few meters to ensure the nose wheel is straightened.
- [ ] **Nose Wheel Lock:** Set the nose wheel brake to the "ON" (horizontal) locked position.
- [ ] **Landing Gear Safety:** Open the landing gear safety catch.
